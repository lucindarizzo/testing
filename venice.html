📝 Overview

Venice, the floating city of canals and bridges, offers a magical experience unlike anywhere else. We'll enjoy gondola rides, strolls through charming alleys, historic architecture, and delicious Venetian cuisine. Perfect for photography, shopping, and relaxing by the water.
